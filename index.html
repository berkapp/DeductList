Data autosaves to this browser (localStorage) as you type or add/delete rows. Monthly JAN–DEC figures start blank — enter them yourself as deductions actually happen. Pending Deduction = amount owed at creation. Total Deducted = sum of JAN–DEC entered so far. Balance Deduction = Pending Deduction − Total Deducted.
Serial No.: every deduction row has its own unique serial number (shown as the 2nd column, e.g. 001, 002, 003). Serial numbers are assigned in creation order — deleting a row removes its number permanently (no reuse). To post a payment directly: click ⚡ Pay by Serial No. in the toolbar, enter the number, choose the month, and enter the amount. For bulk entry: export to Excel, fill in monthly amounts next to the correct Serial Numbers, then import back.
